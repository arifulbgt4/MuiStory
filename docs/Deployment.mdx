# Deployment

> **Server configuration varies depending on its intended purpose. Please follow your service provider's instructions to ensure that your server is configured correctly.**

## Vercel

The easiest way to deploy your Next.js app is to use the [Vercel](http://vercel.com/) Platform from the creators of Next.js.<br />
Check out [Next.js deployment documentation](https://nextjs.org/docs/pages/building-your-application/deploying) for more details.
